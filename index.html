About This Journey

Refine is a student-run club dedicated to helping learners build skills in finance, economics, and programming.
Our goal is to make learning finance and coding fun, practical, and empowering for young minds and help them feel confident in these skills. We are based in Canton, Michigan and host bootcamps, events, and sessions!

Founders and Presidents

Sara Bhatnagar

Hi! My name is Sara Bhatnagar, and I am a rising senior at Salem High School. As the Programming Lead, Co-President, and Co-Founder of Refine, I am excited to help students discover how technology can be used to solve real-world problems. I am especially interested in programming and enjoy finding ways to connect technical skills with topics like economics and personal finance.
Through Refine, I hope to make learning both engaging and practical by introducing students to concepts that can help them in the future. My goal is to create a welcoming environment where students can explore new ideas, build useful skills, and have fun while learning.
Outside of school, I enjoy baking, going on walks, and working on programming projects!

Navya Dwivedi

Hello! My name is Navya Dwivedi, and I am currently a rising senior at Washtenaw International High School. I am the Co-President, Co-Founder, and Finance Lead of ReFine. In my free time, I enjoy playing volleyball, kayaking, and listening to music while going on walks.
I am passionate about expanding children's access to education and fostering inclusive communities, which is part of the reason why I co-founded ReFine. Through this organization, I hope to support our community by providing opportunities for youth to stay involved, remain curious, and continue learning. My goal is to help create a welcoming environment where every student feels encouraged to explore new interests and develop valuable skills.
I hope to grow our ReFamily one child at a time!
